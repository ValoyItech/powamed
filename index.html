About Us

PowaMed is a proudly South African medical equipment supplier with years of experience in delivering top-quality healthcare solutions to both the public and private sectors. We specialize in a broad range of medical categories that meet international standards. Our expertise includes:

Single-use devices like syringes and catheters to ensure sterile, disposable solutions for patient safety.

Implantables such as hip prostheses and pacemakers that support long-term patient recovery and mobility.

Imaging equipment including ultrasound and CT scanners to deliver precise diagnostics.

Medical equipment like anesthesia machines, patient monitors, and hemodialysis machines for high-performance care.

Software solutions for computer-aided diagnostics and enhanced healthcare workflow.

In vitro diagnostics including glucometers and HIV tests for rapid, reliable results.

Personal protective equipment: masks, gowns, gloves, and more for staff and patient safety.

Surgical and laboratory instruments built for precision and reliability.

Technical support, service, and repairs to maintain uninterrupted operation of medical equipment.
